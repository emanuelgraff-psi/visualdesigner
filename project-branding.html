Modern Visual Identity

Like Contábil

An accounting brand created for freelancers and service providers, focused on simplicity and a positive user experience.

Overview

This branding project was developed for an established accounting company called Congel. The new brand, Like Contábil, was created to attract a fresh client base beyond Congel’s existing customers. The concept was clear: a 100% online accounting platform designed for freelancers, MEIs (Brazilian micro-entrepreneurs), and service providers such as electricians and painters. The goal was to offer an easy, lightweight, and affordable solution for generating and managing invoices.

Role

Brand strategy, visual identity, presentation design.

Timeline

4 weeks

Tools

Illustrator, Photoshop, Figma

The Challenge

Creating a recognizable and flexible visual system

With well-defined personas, the project focused on delivering something tangible, simple, and memorable. The name and logo were crafted to convey positivity—Like Contábil features a “Thumbs Up” symbol, representing optimism and approachability.
