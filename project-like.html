Mitsubishi Campaign

Building a bold and persuasive campaign system that translates energy, clarity, and premium perception across digital and presentation touchpoints.

Client

Mitsubishi

Role

Graphic Designer / UX/UI

Scope

Campaign assets, landing page, presentation

Year

2026

Overview

What this project was about

This project focused on creating a stronger visual direction for a campaign that needed to feel dynamic, premium, and immediately clear to the audience.

Goal

A sharper communication system

The intention was to improve visual consistency, reinforce the message hierarchy, and make the campaign assets feel more intentional across different formats.

Challenge

Balancing impact and clarity

The main challenge was creating something expressive without making the communication feel overloaded. Strong visuals were important, but hierarchy and readability had to stay sharp.

Approach

System before decoration

The design direction centered on structured layout blocks, controlled contrast, and a repeatable visual rhythm that could scale into multiple assets and future campaign pieces.

Execution

Turning concept into deliverables

Once the visual direction was defined, it was applied to presentation layouts, campaign pieces, and digital assets, all with the same visual logic and communication intent.

Outcome

A more cohesive campaign presence

The result was a cleaner and more persuasive campaign system, with stronger visual consistency and a more confident overall brand impression.
